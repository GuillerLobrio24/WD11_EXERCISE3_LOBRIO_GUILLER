Simala Shrine, Cebu

The Miraculous Castle Church!!!

Simala Shrine

Simala Church is a prestigious castle-like structure that tourists flock to.
Home to the Monastery of the Holy Eucharist, people from all over the world
visit this pristine church to witness traditions and religious gatherings.
Many Catholic devotees include Simala Church to their list of must-visit tourist spots in Cebu.
Simala Shrine, popularly known as The Monastery of the Holy Eucharist,
is situated in Barangay Lindogon, Sibonga, Cebu.
It is among the most visited religious places in the Philippines.
